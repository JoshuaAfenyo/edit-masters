You can use a notebook or MS Word to write your answers down. There are no right or wrong answers. Just practice. Please refer to [AN ADVANCED ENGLISH GRAMMAR](http://www.gutenberg.org/files/45814/45814-h/45814-h.htm#III-3) for reference.
